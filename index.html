INITIATION

Receive Your Beans

Every ritual begins with an offering. €10 grants entry and 100g of Canarian coffee beans — not a purchase, but an invitation to participate.

"First, you hold the raw material. Then you learn what it can become."

Browse local honey, volcanic salt, ceramics. The journey is paid; the experience begins.

EDUCATION & CHOICE

Understand Then Choose

Walk through centuries of coffee culture in the Canary Islands. From Guanche farming tools to modern roasting techniques.

"To influence coffee, you must first understand its journey."

Then decide: taste today's curated filter coffee immediately, or continue to create your own.

CREATION

Brew Amidst Art

The final act: your beans, your hands. Choose Belgian siphon or V60. Receive barista guidance, not service.

"Here, you don't order coffee — you complete it."

Surrounded by Canarian art on one side, Montana del Mojón volcano on the other. Seven tables for seven simultaneous rituals.

PHILOSOPHY

LAVA is not a café. It's a space where coffee becomes medium — for education, creation, and connection with Canarian culture and landscape.

LOGISTICS

90-minute experience. 14 guests maximum. Walk-ins only. Last entry 14:00. Children welcome (€5 under 12).

LOCATION

Calle Volcán 15, at the foot of Montana del Mojón. Between tourist centers and authentic Tenerife.

ART PROGRAM

Rotating exhibition of Canarian artists (public domain). Volcanic landscapes, coffee culture, island life.
